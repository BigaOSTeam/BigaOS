tailscale.setup_title=Setup Instructions
tailscale.setup_instructions=1. Create a free Tailscale account at tailscale.com\n2. In the admin console, go to Settings > Keys and generate an Auth Key ("Reusable" for headless devices)\n3. Enable MagicDNS — admin console > DNS > toggle "MagicDNS" on. This lets every device on your tailnet reach the boat by its machine name instead of an IP address.\n4. Paste the key into the field below and save the hostname (default: bigaos)\n5. From your phone (with Tailscale installed and connected), open the BigaOS app and connect to http://<hostname>:3000 — for example http://bigaos:3000. The hostname is stable; no need to update it after IP changes.\n6. If using subnet routing, approve the routes in the Tailscale admin console under the machine's route settings.

tailscale.auth_key_desc=Your Tailscale auth key (starts with "tskey-auth-")

tailscale.hostname_desc=Name for this boat on your Tailscale network. With MagicDNS enabled, your phone reaches the server at http://<hostname>:3000 — paste that into the BigaOS app's setup instead of an IP.
